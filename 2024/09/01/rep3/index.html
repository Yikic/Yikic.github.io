Chat with Joker Xue

Joker Xue is one of my favorite singers, and he has a huge fanbase in China, thanks to his unique voice and captivating songs. Inspired by his popularity, I decided to create an interactive experience where fans can chat with him. To achieve this, I combined the power of Llama 3.1 with a TTS model. For the text-to-speech component, I chose gpt-sovits, which is known for its impressive voice quality.
