Education

I pursued a bachelor’s degree in Mechatronic Engineering at Guangdong University of Technology, driven by a childhood dream of building my own robot. However, during my sophomore year, I realized that mechatronics alone wasn’t enough to achieve my broader goals. It is then did I develop a strong interest in machine learning and began watching toturials in youtube and bilibili, with the intention of further exploring this field in statistics and computer science.

Graduation Project

For my graduation project, I focused on the path planning performance of mobile robots using various learning algorithms. I evaluated the effectiveness of modern algorithms, including LSTM, CAE-LSTM, and Waypoint Planning Networks, and compared them to traditional algorithms like A* and Dijkstra, creating a comprehensive benchmark.
